Probabilistic ML · Research

Scout-Guided HMC

A novel sampler that adds lightweight "scout" evaluations before each Hamiltonian trajectory, steering momentum away from low-probability regions before committing to a full trajectory. Activates only when the chain is struggling — zero overhead otherwise.

  • 4.7× ESS improvement over baseline HMC on Rosenbrock (639 vs 135)
  • Only 6.3% of steps trigger scouting; 6× ESS/evaluation gain
  • Metropolis–Hastings correction retained for asymptotic unbiasedness
Python NumPy MCMC Bayesian Inference
AI Safety · NLP

Safety Metrics for LLMs

A reproducible evaluation framework for safety-critical LLM behavior across vulnerable user groups — focusing on content safety, minor exposure controls, and elder-targeted scam detection in Gemini and Llama.

  • 0–3 scoring rubric across refusal rate, safety alignment, intervention timing

  • Identifies overcorrection vs. under-blocking failure modes

Computer Vision · Research Tools

Anxiety Behaviour Detection in Children

A computer vision pipeline that helps psychology researchers automate behavioral coding of child anxiety in video experiments — replacing days of manual annotation with a minutes-long automated workflow integrated into a web app.

  • 584× faster labeling: 600+ videos in ~30 hours vs. multi-year effort
  • Detects child/parent/stranger with YOLOv8; tracks posture with YOLO Pose
  • Temporal freeze detection (≥2 sec stillness); outputs fear-coded CSV

Data Science · Economics

Economic Modelling of U.S. Farmer Productivity

Uncovering relationships between farmer age demographics and agricultural production across 3,000+ U.S. counties from 1997 onwards, with inflation-adjusted time series analysis.

  • Younger farmers cluster in high-productivity counties; older in low-productivity
  • Older farming population most pronounced in regions with abundant public land
  • Inflation adjustment reveals nominal figures mask real productivity trends
